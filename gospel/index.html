Welcome to Gospel of St. Luke Church


Gospel of St. Luke strives to blend tradition and innovation, the historical and the contemporary. Our worship services have a quality that echoes through the ages and yet, speaks to the present and points to the future.

Our Mission

At the Gospel of St. Luke Church, our mission is to always recruit souls for Christ which is achieved by training and educating believers in the Word of God and in the ways of Christ.

What We Do?

We use both the Old Testament and the Gospels as the foundation for our faith and the inspiration for our daily lives. We put those teachings into practice at home and at work.
